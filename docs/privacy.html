Privacy Policy

1. Core Commitment

Privacy Guardian: Offline Edition is designed with a "Privacy First, Privacy Only" philosophy. The application functions entirely on your device and does not transmit personal data to external servers managed by the developers.

2. Data Processing

All data analyzed by the App Auditor, Device Monitor, Hardware Sentry, and Network Shield is processed locally in real-time. This data is never uploaded, shared, or sold. Any logs generated (such as Hardware Access History) are stored exclusively in the app's secure private storage on your device.

3. Third-Party Services (Ads)

To support development, the app displays non-intrusive banner ads via Google AdMob. To maximize your privacy:

  • Ads are configured as Non-Personalized.
  • No user-identifiable data from Privacy Guardian's monitoring features is shared with AdMob.
  • The app can be used 100% ad-free with a one-time permanent purchase.

4. Permissions

The app requests granular permissions (like Usage Stats or Battery Stats) solely to provide the privacy monitoring features described. These permissions are only used when the app is active or when background monitoring is explicitly enabled by the user.

5. Contact

Since the app is offline and does not collect data, we have no way to identify you. If you have questions about this policy, please open a public issue on our GitHub repository.
